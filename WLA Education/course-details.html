Course Objectives

This comprehensive web development bootcamp is designed to take you from beginner to job-ready full-stack developer in just 12 weeks. You'll gain hands-on experience with the most in-demand technologies used by professional developers today.

By the end of this course, you will be able to build complete web applications from scratch, understand modern development workflows, and deploy your projects to production servers. We focus on practical skills that employers value, including version control with Git, responsive design principles, and API integration.

Our project-based approach ensures you'll graduate with a professional portfolio showcasing your abilities. Whether you're looking to start a new career in tech or upgrade your existing skills, this course provides the foundation you need to succeed in today's competitive job market.

Who Should Attend

This course is ideal for beginners with no prior coding experience, career changers looking to enter the tech industry, or professionals who want to expand their digital skillset. Basic computer literacy is required, but no programming knowledge is assumed.

We've designed the curriculum to be accessible while still challenging enough for those with some HTML/CSS familiarity. If you're motivated to learn and willing to dedicate 15-20 hours per week to coursework and projects, you'll find this program rewarding and career-transforming.
